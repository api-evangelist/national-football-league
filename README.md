# National Football League (national-football-league)

The National Football League (NFL) is the premier professional American football league, comprised of 32 franchises divided between the AFC and NFC conferences. The NFL produces the Super Bowl and is the most-watched sports league in the United States. The league does not operate a public developer portal; statistical and play-by-play data feeds are licensed to partners (notably AWS, Amazon Web Services Next Gen Stats, Sportradar, and Genius Sports) and are not offered as a self-serve developer API.
